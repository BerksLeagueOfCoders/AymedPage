About Us: The EightBitZoo

We ar;e an Online Gaming Organization run by Airii "Pxlcharm Coad and Cassie "KittyKass" Bowlen. We hold events throughout the year as well as stream online and attend conventions around the country.


This year we will be hosting our EightBitLAN event in 2019. This is a BYOC (Bring your own console/computer) 3 day Event. There will be all kinds of games and tournaments to enter as well as free play during the event. We will also have a variety of Vendors and sponsors gracing the event with their wares. This event will be opened to the public as well. The event will open at 10:00 AM Friday morning and end 10:00 AM Sunday Morning.


This year we are very pleased to announce that Nintendo of America will be attending with their own booth to give out free goodies to attendees. All proceeds raised this event will go towards The Children's Hospital of Philadelphia for the patients medical bills/equipment. So far we have over 150 confirmed attendees! This is shaping up to be a huge event!
